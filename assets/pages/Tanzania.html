History & Milestones

2004

Inception & First Footprint

Established our primary outreach clinic infrastructure to target underserved urban communities with affordable, quality medical diagnostics.

2014

Polyclinic Network Expansion

Scaled our operational footprint outside Dar es Salaam, opening primary health care networks in Mwanza, Arusha, and Dodoma to expand national coverage.

2021

Advanced Diagnostic Integration

Upgraded our hub facilities with advanced modern medical imaging systems, introducing CT-Scans, digital radiology, and centralized pathology labs.

2025

Digital Health Framework

Launched unified Electronic Health Records (EHR) systems across all Tanzanian polyclinics, enabling seamless inter-facility patient data transfer and care management.
